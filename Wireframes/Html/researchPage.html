Chart of References:


Navigation Bar:

Reference for our navigation bar was taken from Apple. Apple has a navigation bar with a blurry effect (left image) whereas our team has used a solid background but with a little less opacity (right image) which enables the viewers to focus more onto the main objective ie. printers.

Home Page:

Reference for our homepage was taken from Cannon. Cannon has a homepage which has a top section which enables users to learn about their products and the section below shows featured products (left image). Our team has provided the top section with a new product that has many latest features (right image). This helps to peak interest among users. Also, the section below from the top section, on our homepage, has featured products as well but with a slider to help users to focus on one product at a time.

Buy Page:

Reference for our buy page was taken from Amazon. Amazon has presented its products with its image, name, description, price and other details (left image) while our team has presented its products with simplicity by only presenting a image, name, price and buttons to buy or add to cart (right image). The description for the product is available as well and it can accessed by clicking on the printers image and name which redirects to a webpage containing all information about the respective products.

About Us Page:

Reference for our about us page was taken from Karizma Marketing. Kariz Marketing has each team member's image with no background and names included (left image). Our team also designed our about us page with a photo with no background and names but also an anchor on a text which redirects viewers to the portfolio of the respective team member (right image).

Footer:

Reference for our footer was taken from Chobani. Chobani has a section to provide email in order to get their news and other information (left image) which our team has utilized as well but also included our logo and text with anchors that redirects to all of our main respective webpages (right image).

Description Page:

Reference for our description page was taken from Cannon as well. Canon has an image of the respective printer with its description below (left image). Our team has also provided the webpage with the image of the respective printer with its description listed out but also price and buy-button to make the buying process faster for the users (right image).

Portfolio Page:

Reference for our portfolio page was taken from Behance. Behance contains different creative ideas from different artists. The referenced image is one of the many ideas from behance where the respective artist has created a simple, yet beautiful portfolio (left image). One's picture, words and contact information at the left with education, skills and certifications on the middle and hobbies and socials at the right. Our team has used the ideas but simplified the middle section with simple words and stars on skills to show the experience and understanding of the respective skills (right image).
